Lymph massage 90 min
145,90 €

The lymphatic system is the body's sewer and purification system. It flushes out debris and harmful substances in our bodies, transports around immune cells and maintains our important fluid balance.


When the lymphatic system flows properly, we also feel good, it makes us feel light, mobile and energetic. When the lymphatic system is sluggish and congested instead, we can feel lethargic, heavy and tired. In a lymphatic massage, we work with slow and firm grips in the direction of the lymph as well as loosening grips to facilitate and improve the flow of the lymph.
